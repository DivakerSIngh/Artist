About Us

Hey, I’m looking for a drummer for Sunday service. Let me know if anyone is interested.

We are having a youth revival and I don’t know of many worship teams or dance teams that I could invite to participate. Can anyone help?

I’m having a wedding reception and need a Christian DJ. Do you know of any?

I’m free Sunday if there’s a church looking for an organist.

These are just a few questions that we’ve stumbled across that always made us think:

“Why is there not a better way for connections to be made within the praise and worship community?”

So, we have created a better way. Judah’s Link exists to create a more convenient way for Christian church artists to connect with opportunities to use their gifts and skills through our systems of profile creation and ad placement. We also provide a way for churches, organizations, groups, and individuals to place ads, fill positions and meet their ministry and event needs.

Our goal is to strengthen ministry. Whether it is an artist finding a position, someone finding an artist; even if it is someone finding equipment needed in worship ministry in our Sell and Shop or an artist

learning from another artist in Judah’s community, ministry is strengthened. There are Christian church artists that possess many gifts and the Christian community hold many opportunities. We seek to bring the two together through our unique profile linking system with a greater goal of molding ministry.
